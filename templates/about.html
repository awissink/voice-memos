it all started with a class...

columbia student amelia wissink was in a web design studio class when she was tasked with creating a useful website using generative AI. she thought for weeks about something that could be useful for her classmates to no avail, until one of her friends said:

"wow, I hate listening to voice memos."

a lightbulb went off, and transcribify was born!
